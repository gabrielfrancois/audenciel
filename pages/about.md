# About                    
                           
## Organization            
                           
This challenge is based on the **REFIT** dataset, which includes electricial consumption at aggregate and appliance level.
With the help of **AUDENSIEL**, this dataset has been annotated and adapted for this challenge and to support their **EcoSmartGrid** project.
